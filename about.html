About me

Hey there! I'm Rocio, originally from Argentina. I've found my new home in this cute little city in The Netherlands called Scheemda. At the moment coding at Bijvoorbeeld Zo and looking forward to new experiences to keep growing my coding skills.

I juggle between making cool stuff on the frontend with React and React Native, and dabbling in the backend using Node.js. And hey, I've played around with a bunch of other tech stuff like Ruby, RoR, Express.js, and a lot more.

Back in 2021, I waved goodbye to Nutrition and jumped onto the coding train. And I loved it! Turning ideas into code is awesome!

Ever since then, I've been on this awesome rollercoaster of learning, building, and enjoying those tricky code puzzles.

You'll catch me with a book in one hand, a cup of mate in the other. I also love to keep myself active, I love walking with my 2 lovely dogs or going to they gym.
